TL;DR

Who

Comms: Steers Shopify's story across earned, owned, shared, and paid channels across 7 international markets.

GA: Drives policy engagement and reduces regulatory friction across the EU, UK, and key APAC markets.

What

Comms: Position Shopify as the product-led company powering commerce for entrepreneurs and enterprises, laddering up to the H1 priorities.

GA: Translate those priorities into policy positions and advocacy that reduces regulatory friction, defends merchant interests, and builds Shopify's political equity.

Why

Comms: Cement Shopify's AI narrative, showcase product strength, and highlight merchant and partner impact in growth markets.

GA: Europe is the most complex regulatory environment for digital commerce. Policy decisions in H1 2026 will determine whether merchants can scale efficiently.

Global Hero Narratives

Shopify makes entrepreneurship more common

Fundamentally, we believe more entrepreneurs make the world a better place. Shopify champions entrepreneurship and supports every entrepreneur, big or small.

Shopify is built for the complexity of commerce

Merchants bet on Shopify to build the future, because we're the most unified platform and we innovate faster than anyone else. With millions of merchants and billions of transactions processed, no platform understands commerce diversity like Shopify.

Shopify is the foundation of agentic commerce

We've spent decades building the infrastructure that lets businesses thrive, taking raw technological power and turning it into tools for millions of merchants. Now we're extending that foundation into the AI era.

H1 Priorities

5 Big Bets

1

Land our differentiated AI story

Big plays

  • Agentic commerce sustained storytelling — cross-market outreach framing AI in practical, merchant-first terms (podcasts, op-eds, thought leadership)
  • Executive positioning — 2–3 speaker slots per region (Shoptalk Luxe ✅ / MPE ✅ / Stop Forward ✅ / Reuters AI Momentum 🎯)
  • AI Commerce Index & Heatmap — show where Sidekick usage is clustering and prove adoption is growing
  • Proactive myth-busting advocacy — demystify agentic commerce for policymakers; position it as SME infrastructure, not Big Tech

What success looks like

Shopify is recognised as the leader in AI and agentic commerce. Coverage connects Sidekick, Agentic Storefronts, and other AI products to real merchant outcomes.

Scale

~25 earned placements · 10–12 briefings · ~12 shared/owned pieces · 4 speaking engagements · 8 merchant stories

2

Amp up product storytelling

Big plays

  • Winter Edition '26 (Q1) — momentum storytelling targeting earned + podcast placements across markets ✅
  • Spring Edition '26 (29 April) — integrated, cross-channel launch: hero product deep-dives, strategic reach, local spokesperson thought leadership
  • Shopify's security story — proactive pitching of Dunbar to key security media and podcasts internationally (The Control Layer ✅)

What success looks like

Shopify shifts perception from "the entrepreneurship company" to also being "the product company." Coverage reflects platform depth and deep understanding of the complexity of commerce.

Scale

~12 earned placements · 8–10 briefings · ~6 shared/owned pieces · 4 merchant stories

3

Directly advocate for Shopify + merchants

Big plays

  • Advocacy (lead) — direct engagement with EU institutions, UK DBT, and capitals on core priorities: Entrepreneurship, AI & agentic, Privacy & cookies, Trade, Platform liability, Payments
  • Scale influence — leverage policy coalitions; own key policy moments (roundtables, SME forums, embassy engagement)
  • Amplify through comms — issues hijacking with fast, opinionated reactions to government policy moves on trade, regulation, and AI
  • Merchant storytelling — profile standout merchants (e.g. Cabaia in FR, DFYNE & Jaded London in UK)

What success looks like

Shopify recognised as the best place to build. Shape the rules of agentic commerce & cross-border trade; position entrepreneurship as essential economic infrastructure.

Scale

~60 stakeholder meetings · ~40 policy consultations · 5 roundtables · ~18 earned placements · 4–6 briefings · ~16 shared/owned pieces · 10 merchant stories

4

Own the entrepreneur-to-enterprise story

Big plays

  • Sustained thought leadership — constant drumbeat of exec thought leadership across earned media (Business Builders ✅ / Dolcesalato ✅ + targets: Sifted Summit, Enterprise Times, Il Caffettino, RetailBiz, Der Handel)
  • Pulse surveys (quarterly) — leverage Harris Poll insights for media opportunities, paired with proactive commentary from Deann & Shaun
  • INTL Uncertainty campaign — support milestone campaign; explore report-led campaign unpacking the uncertainty narrative with local nuances for 3–6 months of earned, owned, and paid activation

What success looks like

The market understands Shopify's tools support solopreneurs through to the world's most sophisticated brands. We cement our position as the engine of entrepreneurial growth.

Scale

~14 earned placements · 6–8 briefings · ~12 shared/owned pieces · 2 speaking engagements · 8 merchant + partner stories

5

Leverage data to demonstrate our impact

Big plays

  • Economic impact report/data (TBC) — markets primed and ready to activate
  • Targeted product trend pitches — local market data for specific story angles (e.g. "little luxuries" in UK, price sensitivities in DE, cherry blossom season trends in JP)
  • Annual Merchant Survey (AI data) — explore external release of Sidekick usage, AI adoption stats, and merchant AI use-cases
  • Policy & advocacy leverage — use proprietary data to shape EU and global debates on entrepreneurship, AI, and trade

What success looks like

Shopify's proprietary data shapes the commerce agenda. Media and policymakers see Shopify as the most credible lens on entrepreneurship at scale.

Scale

~12 earned placements · ~12 shared/owned pieces
